Weight Loss Program

Our Weight Loss Program combines targeted cardio, metabolic circuits, and nutrition coaching to help you shed fat while preserving lean muscle mass. This comprehensive approach ensures sustainable results through a balanced mix of high-intensity interval training (HIIT), strength exercises, and dietary guidance tailored to your lifestyle.

Participants will receive personalized meal plans, weekly progress tracking, and access to our expert trainers who provide ongoing support and adjustments to your routine. Whether you're looking to lose a few pounds or embark on a significant transformation, this program is designed to build healthy habits that last.

What you'll get

  • Personalized calorie and meal planning with macro tracking
  • Fat-burning workouts with progressive intensity and variety
  • Body composition tracking, weekly check-ins, and motivation coaching
  • Access to group classes and one-on-one sessions
  • Educational resources on sustainable weight management

Program Duration & Pricing

Available in 60-day (1,500 ETB) or 90-day (3,000 ETB) plans.
