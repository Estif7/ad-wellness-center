About Us

Coach Amanuel – Personal Trainer & Fitness Instructor

With over five years of hands-on experience in the fitness industry, I specialize in helping individuals transform their bodies through structured, realistic, and results-driven training. My approach is built around understanding each client’s unique needs and creating personalized workout programs that are both effective and sustainable.

I’ve worked with a wide range of clients—from beginners looking to build confidence in the gym to experienced individuals aiming to break plateaus. My expertise includes bodybuilding, weight loss, strength training, and pre- and post-rehabilitation support, ensuring that every client trains safely while progressing toward their goals.

  • Fitness Instructor & Fitness Management
  • Physiotherapy and Rehabilitation Guidance

Dagmawi Yohannes – Fitness Coach & Nutrition Expert

I believe fitness doesn’t have to be complicated to work it just needs to be consistent, structured, and right for you. As a certified fitness coach with over two years of experience, I focus on helping clients build strength, lose fat, and stay consistent without overwhelming them.

I specialize in functional fitness, strength training, and basic conditioning helping clients improve performance in everyday life, not just in the gym. I also emphasize proper form and injury prevention, making sure every movement is done safely and efficiently.
